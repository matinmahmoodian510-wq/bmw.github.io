About BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 and is headquartered in Munich, Bavaria.

BMW is known for its performance and luxury vehicles, and is one of the best-selling luxury automakers in the world. The company's slogan is "The Ultimate Driving Machine".

Popular BMW Models

BMW 3 Series

  • Compact executive car
  • Available as sedan, wagon, and gran turismo
  • Engine options: 2.0L to 3.0L
  • Horsepower: 255 to 382 hp

BMW 5 Series

  • Executive car
  • Available as sedan and wagon
  • Engine options: 2.0L to 4.4L
  • Horsepower: 248 to 523 hp

BMW X5

  • Mid-size luxury SUV
  • Available with various engine options
  • All-wheel drive standard
  • Horsepower: 335 to 617 hp

BMW Technology

BMW is known for its innovative technologies including:

  • iDrive infotainment system
  • EfficientDynamics for better fuel economy
  • xDrive all-wheel drive system
  • Advanced driver assistance systems
  • Electric and hybrid vehicle technology (BMW i series)
